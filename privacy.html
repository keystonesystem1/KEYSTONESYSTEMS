Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how KEYSTONESYSTEMS (“Keystone,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, and protects information when you visit the Keystone Systems corporate website at keystonestack.com (the “Website”).

01Scope

This Privacy Policy covers the Website only. It does not cover information collected by the software products Keystone Systems builds and operates — each product has its own privacy policy that governs data handled within that product.

Our product privacy policies are available at:

  • INSPEKTiT — inspektit.io/privacy

If you are a user of any Keystone product, the data you submit to that product is governed by that product's privacy policy, not this one.

02Information We Collect on the Website

2.1Information You Provide

When you fill out a contact form, request information, or otherwise communicate with us through the Website, we may collect:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your company or firm name
  • Any message content you submit

We do not ask for or collect sensitive personal information (such as government ID numbers, financial account details, or health information) through the Website.

2.2Information Collected Automatically

When you visit the Website, our hosting provider and analytics tools may automatically collect limited technical information, including:

  • IP address (usually anonymized)
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Pages viewed and time spent on the Website
  • Referring website
  • General geographic location (country or region, derived from IP)

This information is aggregated and used to understand how visitors use the Website. We do not combine it with personally identifying information from contact form submissions unless necessary to respond to a specific inquiry.

2.3Cookies

The Website may use cookies and similar technologies for basic functionality and analytics. You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some Website features but will not prevent you from accessing content.

03How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and communications.
  • Provide information about our products when you request it.
  • Operate and improve the Website.
  • Understand Website usage patterns in aggregate (analytics).
  • Detect, prevent, and respond to fraud or security incidents.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

04How We Share Information

Keystone Systems does not sell personal information. We share information only as follows:

  • Service providers. We use third-party services for hosting, email delivery, analytics, and similar functions. These providers have access only to the information needed to perform their function and are contractually required to protect it.
  • Legal requirements. We may disclose information when required by law, court order, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Keystone, our users, or others.
  • Business transfers. If Keystone is involved in a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, information may be transferred as part of that transaction. Any acquirer will be bound by this Privacy Policy or a successor policy of equivalent protection.

05Third-Party Services We Use

The Website relies on a limited set of third-party services:

  • Hosting and analytics — to serve the Website and measure aggregate traffic.
  • Email delivery — to receive and respond to contact form submissions.
  • Scheduling — when you use a scheduling link, that third-party service has its own privacy policy that applies to your interaction with it.

06Data Retention

We retain contact form submissions and related correspondence for as long as needed to respond to your inquiry and for a reasonable period thereafter for record-keeping and legitimate business purposes. Aggregate analytics data may be retained longer in anonymized form.

You may request deletion of your information at any time by contacting legal@keystonestack.com.

07Data Security

We use commercially reasonable safeguards to protect the information we collect through the Website, including encryption in transit, access controls, and secure hosting. No method of transmission or storage is perfectly secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

08Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding information we hold about you:

  • Access. Request a copy of the information we have about you.
  • Correction. Ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion. Ask us to delete your information, subject to legal obligations we may have to retain it.
  • Opt-out. Opt out of certain uses of your information, including marketing communications.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at legal@keystonestack.com. We will respond within the timeframe required by applicable law.

09Children's Privacy

The Website is not directed to children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided information to us, please contact us at legal@keystonestack.com.

10International Visitors

Keystone Systems is based in the United States. If you visit the Website from outside the U.S., your information will be transferred to and processed in the United States. By using the Website, you consent to that transfer and processing.

11Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated policies will be posted on this page with a new “Last updated” date. Material changes will be communicated through a notice on the Website.

12Contact Us

Questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices? Contact us at legal@keystonestack.com.
